Published 4 Jun 2026 · Harshal Dasani

Markets with Harshal is the public commentary identity connected with Harshal Dasani's market notes, videos and day-to-day observations. The focus is not one narrow asset class; it is the connection between price, policy, positioning and psychology.

Core Themes

The main themes are Indian equity markets, sector rotation, IPOs, gold, silver, currency moves, central-bank policy, global liquidity, geopolitics and the behavioral patterns that repeat during market stress and euphoria.

How This Blog Fits

The blog gives longer context to the same themes that appear in media interviews and quotes. The tracker is the archive; the blog is the place for organized notes, frameworks and explanations.
