Harshal Dasani at INVasset PMS

Business Head at INVasset PMS, Mumbai. Markets professional with 10+ years in Indian equities. CFA candidate, CA Level II. Featured in 1,045+ media pieces across 8 languages.

Harshal Dasani is the Business Head at INVasset PMS, a SEBI-registered portfolio management services firm headquartered in Mumbai with offices in Gurugram, Ludhiana, Ahmedabad and Dubai. He leads business strategy, markets commentary and client engagement across the firm's equity, AIF and discretionary mandates.

With more than a decade in Indian equity markets, Harshal sits at the intersection of fundamental analysis, macro lenses and behavioural finance. He is a CFA candidate with the CFA Institute and has cleared Level II of CA with the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI).
