About
A reader of cycles, psychology, and time.

I'm Harshal Dasani — Business Head at INVasset PMS, with over ten years of experience in Indian equity markets.

I spend most of my days trying to make sense of how the world's biggest forces — central banks, geopolitics, capital flows, human behavior — show up in stock prices. I've come to believe that the best investors aren't the loudest forecasters; they're the most disciplined readers of cycles, psychology, and time.

My approach is part numbers, part narrative. Fundamental analysis tells me what a business is worth. Technical and macro lenses tell me when the market is willing to listen. Behavioral finance reminds me that everyone — including me — is more biased than they think. I try to hold all three in mind at once.

I'm a CFA candidate and have cleared Level II of the Chartered Accountancy program — credentials that have taught me as much about how to think as what to know.

Outside the desk, you'll find me sharing views across India's leading financial publications — The Economic Times, Mint, Moneycontrol, Business Standard, Financial Express, Business Today, Outlook Money, Times of India, Forbes, PTI, and Reuters — and on television via Good Returns, CNBC Bajar Gujarati, and Moneycontrol. I also publish long-form market commentary on YouTube and X as Markets with Harshal.
