About
A reader of cycles, psychology, and time.

I'm Harshal Dasani — Business Head at INVasset PMS, with over ten years in Indian equity markets. I spend most days trying to make sense of how the world's biggest forces — central banks, geopolitics, capital flows, human behavior — show up in stock prices.

My approach is part numbers, part narrative. Fundamentals tell me what a business is worth; technical and macro lenses tell me when the market is willing to listen; behavioral finance reminds me that everyone — including me — is more biased than they think. I'm a CFA candidate and have cleared Level II of CA.

Outside the desk, you'll find me sharing views across The Economic Times, Mint, Moneycontrol, Business Standard, Financial Express, Business Today, Outlook Money, Times of India, Forbes, PTI and Reuters — and on television via Good Returns, CNBC Bajar Gujarati and Moneycontrol. I publish long-form commentary on YouTube and X as Markets with Harshal.
