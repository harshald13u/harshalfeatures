Privacy Policy

A short, honest summary of what this site collects, what it doesn't, and your rights.

Last updated: 8 June 2026

Short version. This is a static personal website. It does not ask for your name, email, or phone number. It does not have logins, forms, or accounts. It does not run advertising trackers. The only data it touches is functional — what's needed to deliver the page and remember your theme & language choice.

1. Who runs this site

The site at harshaldasani.pages.dev is operated personally by Harshal Dasani, based in India. Acting as the data fiduciary under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act), the author is responsible for this policy.

2. What the site collects

DataWhyRetention
IP address & basic request metadata Logged by the hosting provider (Cloudflare Pages) to deliver pages, prevent abuse, and produce aggregate traffic counts As per Cloudflare's policy
Theme preference (hd-theme) Stored in localStorage in your browser so the site remembers light/dark across visits Until you clear it
Language preference (hd-lang) Stored in localStorage in your browser so the site remembers English/Hindi across visits Until you clear it

3. What the site does NOT collect

  • No name, email, phone number, or address.
  • No account, login, or password.
  • No contact forms or sign-ups.
  • No advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, or behavioural profiles.
  • No Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or similar third-party analytics scripts.
  • No payment information.
  • No location data beyond the country-level IP geolocation logged by the host.

4. Cookies & local storage

The site itself does not set any tracking cookies. It uses your browser's localStorage for two preferences only: theme (light/dark) and language (en/hi). These never leave your device.

Your browser may receive functional cookies from the hosting provider (Cloudflare) needed to deliver the site — for example, a security cookie to detect bots. These are first-party in nature and described in Cloudflare's Cookie Policy.

You can clear theme and language preferences at any time by clearing your browser's site data for harshaldasani.pages.dev.

5. Third-party content embedded on the site

Some pages embed or load content from third parties. Those third parties may set their own cookies and collect data under their own policies. The third parties used on this site are:

  • Cloudflare Pages — hosting and CDN. Cloudflare Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — web fonts (Inter, Spectral). Google Privacy Policy.
  • YouTube — video embeds on blog and media pages. YouTube/Google Privacy Policy.
  • X (formerly Twitter) — if any post embeds tweets. X Privacy Policy.
  • TradingView — if any post embeds charts or widgets. TradingView Privacy Policy.

If you want to avoid these, use your browser's privacy controls (third-party cookie blocking, "do not track", or a privacy-focused browser).

6. Server logs

The hosting provider (Cloudflare Pages) logs requests for operational, security, and aggregate-analytics purposes. The author does not maintain a separate logging or analytics layer on top of Cloudflare's defaults. No personally identifiable user profile is constructed.

7. Children

The site is not directed at children under 18. No personal data is knowingly collected from minors.

8. Your rights

Because this site does not store any personal data about you, most data-subject rights are met by design. However, where you are a data principal under the DPDP Act, 2023, the GDPR, or comparable laws, you may:

  • Confirm what (if anything) is held about you;
  • Request correction of inaccurate data;
  • Request erasure;
  • Withdraw any consent you previously gave;
  • Lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Board of India or your local supervisory authority.

To exercise these rights, contact the author via the channels listed on the home page.

9. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when site behaviour changes (for example, if a new third-party embed is added). Updates will be reflected by changing the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the author via the channels listed on the home page. Reasonable requests are typically answered within 30 days.
